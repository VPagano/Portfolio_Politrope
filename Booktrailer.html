Book Trailer - Il visconte dimezzato

Book trailer of Italo Calvino's masterpiece Il visconte dimezzato.

The main character of the book is the personification of the contemporary man who is physical and moral fragmented, in a permanent status of inner contrast between good and evil. All the story is expressing this inner split through three phases. The satirical language of the book, was visually translated through the metaphor of the ballet: simple shapes, moves around creating symmetries and intersections. The 18th century imaginary is shown through the palette composed of warm colors, representing the good half, and cold ones, representing the bad one. The colors show the continuous presence of both half. Audio and video are strictly connected: all the movements are synchronized perfectly creating a synaesthetic relationship while moments of speech guide the interpretation.
