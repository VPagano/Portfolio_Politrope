Spunk: a new edition

Book trailer of Italo Calvino's masterpiece Il visconte dimezzato.

“Spunk: a new edition” is the redesigned of the bootleg demo album realized in 1977 by the English punk rock band The Sex Pistols. The new edition tries to redesign The Sex Pistols imaginary in a contemporary key. Trash trend has been identified as the contemporary counterparts of the Sex Pistols rebellion of the ‘60s. The graphic translation adopted is based on the use of glitch as an image and a font in order to emphasized the strong personality of the band.
